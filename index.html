09Assumptionsâ–¼

  • All calculations use calendar days â€” weekends and public holidays are not excluded.
  • Same-day departures are flagged but included â€” CX must call the depot to confirm.

  • A maximum of 2 schedule options are shown per depot/port â€” the earliest feasible and the next available.
